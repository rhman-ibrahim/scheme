description how it works

In Scheme everything belongs to a circle!. To create a circle you must have an account (guest/user) and each has its properties.

looks_one

With a user account you create multiple circles, access all of the applications and recover, in case the password was forgotten.

looks_two

With a guest account you create (1) circle, access the "Spaces" application only and your session lasts for (4) hours then ends.

looks_3

You could only communicate with a circle at a time, and to navigate between circles, you have to use the close and open methods.

looks_4

Its suggested to provide the circle's description paragraph in order to be as specific as possible about it's topic and purpose.

looks_5
